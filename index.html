About

Design that earns
its place.

I'm Anhar — a product designer with 8 years across fintech, healthcare, and consumer apps. I care about the unglamorous work: the research session that reframes the problem, the design system decision that saves six engineers two weeks, the handoff doc that actually gets used.

Before going independent in 2023, I led design at Norda Financial and Phaeros Health. I've worked on products used by millions and on zero-to-one builds with teams of three. Both contexts taught me that good design is fundamentally an act of listening.

When I'm not pushing pixels, I'm cycling around Berlin, contributing to Contra Collective, and occasionally writing about cognitive load on Substack.
